General Requirements for California Programs

  • Active, unencumbered California RN license (or ability to obtain)
  • Associate degree in nursing (ADN) or nursing diploma from an accredited program
  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5-3.0 (varies by program)
  • Completion of general education requirements (if not completed in ADN program)
  • Current CPR certification (BLS for Healthcare Providers)
  • Criminal background check and health clearances
  • Professional liability insurance
  • Some programs require prerequisite courses in statistics or anatomy/physiology
