General Requirements for Florida Programs

  • Active, unencumbered Florida RN license
  • Associate degree in nursing (ADN) or nursing diploma from an accredited program
  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5-3.0 (varies by program)
  • Completion of general education requirements
  • Current CPR certification (BLS for Healthcare Providers)
  • Criminal background check and health clearances
  • Professional liability insurance

Financial Aid Resources for Florida Nurses

  • Florida Student Assistance Grant (FSAG): Need-based grant for Florida residents
  • Bright Futures Scholarship: Merit-based scholarship for Florida students
  • Florida Nursing Student Loan Forgiveness Program: Loan forgiveness for nurses working in underserved areas
  • Federal Student Aid (FAFSA): Federal grants, loans, and work-study
  • Employer Tuition Assistance: Many Florida healthcare systems offer tuition reimbursement
